Call for Applications – Become the Selected Pilot Factory for the HumanEnerDIA Field Trials (WASABI Project) – Application Deadline (Extended): 20 February 2026

Dear Manufacturing Industry Leader,

We are pleased to announce a unique opportunity for one forward-looking manufacturing facility in Romania to become the official pilot site of the HumanEnerDIA experiment, developed under the European WASABI programme.
This exclusive pilot will provide the selected facility with full, free-of-charge access to an AI-assisted, human-centric digital energy management system—a technological capability that is typically costly and available only to advanced industrial players.
What is HumanEnerDIA?
HumanEnerDIA integrates our ISO50001-compliant Intel50001 Intelligent Energy Management System with WASABI’s Digital Intelligent Assistant (DIA) to create an innovative, human-centric solution powered by AI, voice interaction, and real-time data analysis. The system helps factory teams monitor and predict energy performance, interact with energy data through voice and text, receive intelligent advice and warnings, and improve resource efficiency and operational awareness.
This experiment is carried out by A Arti Mühendislik (A Plus Engineering, Turkey) together with Green eDIH (Romania).
Why apply to become the pilot host?
• Full deployment of the AI-powered HumanEnerDIA system (free of charge)  
• Onsite installation, configuration, and commissioning  
• Technical visits by A Plus Engineering & Green eDIH  
• Energy performance insights, AI-supported analysis, and KPI reporting  
• Voice- and text-based digital assistant functionalities  
• Visibility through project dissemination across Europe  
No financial contribution is required—participation is fully supported by the WASABI project.
What we expect from the selected factory:
• Willingness to host system installation  
• Availability for technical visits and data collection  
• Participation in feedback and evaluation  
• Collaboration during field trials  
How to apply:
We invite interested manufacturing facilities to express their interest and apply to become the official Romanian pilot site. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for a brief meeting and technical suitability check. Final selection will be made jointly by A Plus Engineering and Green eDIH.
